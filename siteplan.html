Site name

Dry Oar Rafting Adventure

Site purpose with goals

The purpose of the site is to inform the user of what he could expect on a rafting experience
Provide the information of the services we have to offer.
Provide the user the necessary information to contact us and shedule a visit.
Provide a sneek peek of the experience

Target audience

  • Young users between 18 - 25 who are looking for a unique extreme experience.
  • Users that have a medium high social level income who can allocate part of their income
    to spend their free time enjoying the majesty of nature.
  • Users that are students , they have more time to invest than other professions.
  • Very sociable people, who like to spend their free time with their friends and family
  • People who like extreme sports or activities that include adrenaline, movement, agility and dexterity.
